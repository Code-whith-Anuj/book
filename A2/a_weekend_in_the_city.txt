Last month, Peter and Jane went to the city for a weekend. The city was very large and busy. Peter and Jane had a small map of the city. They walked from the train station to the hotel. The hotel was a tall building with a blue sign.

On Saturday morning, they went to the museum. "Can you tell us where the museum is?" Peter asked a woman in the street. The woman was kind. "Go straight, turn left, and walk past the big park," she said. Peter and Jane followed her directions. They found the museum in ten minutes. It was full of interesting things.

After the museum, they had lunch at a small cafe. Peter ordered a sandwich and Jane ordered a salad. The food was very good. "Look at the big bridge over the river!" Jane said. They walked to the bridge and took many photos. The river was very wide and blue.

On Sunday morning, they went to the large park. Many children were playing and some people were walking their dogs. Peter and Jane sat on a bench and watched the people. It was a peaceful morning. They also went to the famous clock tower. The tower was very tall and old. "It's time to go home," Peter said at four o'clock. They went back to the train station and caught their train home. It was a very happy weekend in the city.
