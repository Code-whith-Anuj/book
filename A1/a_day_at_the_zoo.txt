Today is Saturday. Many families are at the zoo. It is a sunny day. I see a big elephant. The elephant is grey. It has a long nose. It is eating an apple. Next, I see the lions. There are three lions. They are sleeping under a tree. They are very big and yellow.

I go to see the monkeys. The monkeys are funny. They are jumping and playing. One monkey has a banana. It is eating the banana. I like the monkeys. Finally, I see the birds. The birds are many colors: red, blue, and green. They are singing. The zoo is a very interesting place. I want to come back next Saturday.
