Anna is in her garden. The sun is hot. She sees a small cat under a tree. The cat is white and grey. It is very small. Anna says, "Hello, little cat." The cat is afraid. It runs behind a big red flower.

Anna goes into her house. She gets a small bowl of milk. She puts the milk on the grass. She waits. After five minutes, the cat comes out. It drinks the milk. Now it is happy. The cat sits next to Anna. Anna feels the cat's soft fur. "You are a nice cat," she says.

Anna looks at the cat. It has a blue collar. There is a name on the collar: Leo. There is also a phone number. Anna takes her phone and calls the number. A woman answers. She says her name is Mrs. Miller. Mrs. Miller is very sad. She says, "Leo is my cat. He is lost. Where is he?"

Anna tells Mrs. Miller her address. Mrs. Miller says, "I am coming now. Thank you very much!" Anna waits with Leo. They play with a yellow ball. Leo is not afraid now. He likes Anna. Anna likes Leo too.

Ten minutes later, a car stops. Mrs. Miller get out of the car. She sees Leo. She is very happy. She hugs her cat. Leo says, "Meow." Mrs. Miller gives Anna some cookies. "Thank you for finding my cat," she says. Anna says, "You are welcome. Leo is a very good cat." Mrs. Miller and Leo go home. Anna is happy. She goes back into her garden. It is a beautiful day.
